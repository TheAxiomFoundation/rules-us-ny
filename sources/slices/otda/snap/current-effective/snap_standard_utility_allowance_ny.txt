New York State Office of Temporary and Disability Assistance, LDSS-5006
"SNAP Public Notice for Thrifty Food Plan Adjustments", effective October 1,
2025:

As of October 1, 2025, the standard allowance for heating/cooling for SNAP
applicant and recipient households residing in New York City is $1,062; for
households residing in either Suffolk or Nassau Counties, it is $988; and for
households residing in any other county of New York State, it is $877.

Model `snap_standard_utility_allowance` as the monthly SNAP heating/cooling
standard utility allowance in USD using New York utility regions
`NY_NYC`, `NY_NAS`, and `NY_ONY` for New York City, Nassau/Suffolk, and other
New York counties, respectively.

Apply only when `snap_utility_allowance_type = SUA`.
