New York State Office of Temporary and Disability Assistance, LDSS-5006
"SNAP Public Notice for Thrifty Food Plan Adjustments", effective October 1,
2025:

As of October 1, 2025, the standard allowance for utilities for SNAP applicant
and recipient households residing in New York City is $419; for households
residing in either Suffolk or Nassau Counties, it is $388; and for households
residing in any other county of New York State, it is $355.

Model `snap_limited_utility_allowance` as the monthly SNAP standard allowance
for utilities in USD using New York utility regions `NY_NYC`, `NY_NAS`, and
`NY_ONY` for New York City, Nassau/Suffolk, and other New York counties,
respectively.

Apply only when `snap_utility_allowance_type = LUA`.
