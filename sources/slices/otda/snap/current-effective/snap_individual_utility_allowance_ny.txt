New York State Office of Temporary and Disability Assistance, LDSS-5006
"SNAP Public Notice for Thrifty Food Plan Adjustments", effective October 1,
2025:

Phone standard utility allowance: $32 in New York City, Nassau/Suffolk, and
Rest of State.

(The OTDA notice presents this as the statewide phone row in the October 1,
2025 Standard Utility Allowance table. SUA values other than the phone
allowance include amounts for water, sewage, and trash collection.)

Model `snap_individual_utility_allowance` as the monthly SNAP phone utility
allowance in USD for a New York SNAP unit when
`snap_utility_allowance_type = TUA` and `snap_utility_region_str = NY`.

For New York, the phone allowance is flat statewide at the amount stated above.
